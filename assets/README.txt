PROFILE PICTURE INSTRUCTIONS
=============================

To add your profile picture to the portfolio:

1. Download your profile photo from LinkedIn:
   - Go to your LinkedIn profile
   - Click on your profile picture
   - Right-click and save the image

2. Rename the file to: profile.jpg

3. Place it in this "assets" folder

The portfolio will automatically display your photo in the About section.

Recommended image specifications:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Size: At least 400x400 pixels
- Aspect ratio: Square (1:1)
